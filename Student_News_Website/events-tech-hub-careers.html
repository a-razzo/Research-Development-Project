Tech Hub Careers Live Opens Recruitment Pathways for Computer Science Students

  • Date: 12 June 2026 at the Tech Hub Innovation Hall.
  • Attendees: Google, Microsoft, ARM, IBM, and other engineering-led employers.
  • Opportunities: Summer placements, graduate software engineer routes, and year-in-industry pipelines.
  • Audience: Computer Science, Cyber Security, and Data Science students.

Edge Hill students are being invited to one of the biggest employability events of the year: Tech Hub Careers Live. The event is designed to connect students directly with hiring managers and engineering leads from global and UK technology brands.

Google will be on-site with teams recruiting for summer placements and permanent software engineering roles, while other employers are advertising opportunities in cloud engineering, AI, cyber defence, and full-stack development. Students can expect practical guidance on interview preparation, portfolio quality, and technical assessments.

Organisers say the goal is to turn networking into real career progression. By the end of the day, students should have clear next steps, direct employer contacts, and a stronger understanding of how to position themselves for current computer science job markets.
